An Introduction to Valheim's Mythological World

Norse mythology is a collection of ancient Scandinavian tales about gods, heroes, and monsters. At its heart lies Yggdrasil, the massive world tree connecting the Nine Realmsâ€”places like Asgard, home of the gods, and Midgard, the human world. Leading the pantheon are Odin, the wise Allfather; Thor, the thunder-wielding warrior; and Loki, the cunning trickster. These stories of fate and bravery shape the world of Valheim.

In Norse mythology, the Nine Worlds are interconnected realms held within the branches and roots of the world tree, Yggdrasil. Each world is home to different beings, from gods and goddesses to giants and humans. In the game Valheim, players are sent to a fictional tenth world, separate from these traditional Nine Worlds, to prove their worth and earn a place in Valhalla.
