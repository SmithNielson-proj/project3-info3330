Odin's Trials: The Bosses of Valheim

Valheim is a savage survival saga in a Norse-inspired open world. As a Viking warrior, I’ve carved my path through its deadly biomes, taking down five of its six legendary bosses: Eikthyr, The Elder, Bonemass, Moder, and Yagluth. The sixth, a mystery I’ve yet to conquer, looms ahead. This site shows my epic journey with my friends through these brutal boss fights.

Valheim’s six brutal bosses guard its harshest realms. After each grueling victory, we haul their trophies back to the stone altar at the world’s heart, claiming their power as our own. From Eikthyr’s thundering antlers to Yagluth’s charred skull, these are the spoils of our blood-soaked triumphs—captured in the shots below as my crew revels in the glory of the hunt. With one final boss ahead of us the fate of the Valheim world rests in our hands.
